Our goal is that every guest feels taken care of, both for hospitality and safety. You will find the people working here positive and open minded, and ready to give of themselves for you as a guest to get the best experience. To achieve this, we are thorough when choosing our crew. Guides and other employees undergo solid training in what it means to be part of the Go Rafting DRY OAR team as hosts. This applies to everything from safety, overall thinking and guest care.

History

We are a local team of writers, photographers, artists, adventures and much more with a shared passion and real life experience from our communities. We tell the stories of the lesser known local destinations, fascinating overlooked detours that will enhance your travel experience and legendary local eateries you won’t find on Google Maps. Nature and adventure travel inspiration • Guides to the best beaches, bars, hotels and events   • Insight into the Dominican Republic’s incredible art and culture, history and heritage   • Meet the locals: in-depth stories about Dominicans doing wonderful things   We publish thoughtful and informed stories in different formats. By telling these stories and helping travellers navigate the best of white Rafting water has to offer, we hope to encourage slower and more mindful travel that has a positive impact.
